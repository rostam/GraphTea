GraphLab V1.0

Graphlab is a modular, pluggable ,easy-to-use and easy-to-develop platform based on scientific mathematical descriptions in graph theory;  capable of working with graphs and graph algorithms. Graphlab is developed at Mathematical Science dept. of Sharif University of Technology. It is written under Java programming language and is licensed under the  GPL.

URL: http://graphlab.sharif.ir
